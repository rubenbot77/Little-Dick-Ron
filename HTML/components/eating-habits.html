EATING HABITS

One of the most surreal and head-scratching discoveries about Florida Gov. Ron DeSantis is what the New Yorker politely called his “indifferent table manners.” The Daily Beast wasn’t so circumspect. They reported that DeSantis had a “propensity to devour food during meetings.”

The Daily Beast interviewed a former staffer who said, “He would sit in meetings and eat in front of people, always like a starving animal who has never eaten before… getting shit everywhere.”
