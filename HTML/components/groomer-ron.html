GROOMER RON

On Truth Social Donald Trump attacked Florida Gov. Ron DeSantis as a “groomer” of young students. The former President showed an incriminating picture, first published by Hill Reporter, of Ron DeSantis surrounded by young high school girls, including one who was holding a beer bottle.

At the time of the photo, DeSantis was a 23-year-old teacher at the Darlington School, a private institution in Rome, Georgia. The photo in question has text over it reading, "Here is Ron DeSanctimonious grooming high school girls with alcohol as a teacher."
